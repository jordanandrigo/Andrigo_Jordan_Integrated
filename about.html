About Me

As a little girl, I took high pleasure in rummaging through boxes of photographs my mother had stashed in the hallway closet. These photographs have allowed me to connect with my past beyond the memories that I have kept in my heart. I grew up, and with that I discovered I can provide the same connection to others with their pasts, as I have with mine.

I would like to invite you along on my journey to becoming more real than reality.
